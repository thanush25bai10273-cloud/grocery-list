1. Project Statement
Grocery List Manager Problem StatementTraditional grocery shopping methods using paper lists or basic digital notes are inefficient and prone to errors. Users struggle with:Disorganized shopping items without
categorization.Difficulty tracking purchased vs. unpurchased items.No persistent storage for recurring shopping lists.Lack of progress tracking during shopping trips.
2.Scope of the Project
The Grocery List Manager is a console-based application that provides a structured approach to grocery shopping. The system allows users to create, manage, and track their shopping lists with categorized items and
persistent data storage, focusing on efficient data handling and user workflow.
3.Target Users
Individual shoppers and families:People who do regular grocery shopping.Users looking to organize their shopping trips efficiently.Individuals who want to track their shopping progress in real-time.
4.High-Level Features
Feature CategoryDescriptionList ManagementCreate and manage multiple grocery lists (CRUD operations).
*Item CategorizationOrganize items into logical categories (e.g., Produce, Dairy, Meats) for systematic shopping
*Purchase TrackingMark items as purchased/unpurchased to aid in progress during a store visit
*Data PersistenceAutomatic saving and loading of all list data to ensure lists are retained between sessions
*Progress MonitoringTrack the completion status of shopping lists (e.g., "5/12 items purchased")
*User-Friendly InterfaceSimple console-based navigation system for quick and intuitive use.
